About this site

A little about
who's behind this

I'm Nicholas — based in De Pere, Wisconsin, where I work in the ecommerce payments industry, coach youth flag football, and try to keep up with everything else life throws at a household.

This site exists as a simple, honest record of what's going on with our family. No algorithm, no engagement metrics — just a place to share updates, document things we're proud of, and give family and friends a window into what we're up to.

When I'm not at work or at practice, I'm probably learning something new (currently: Python and data analysis), working on something around the house, or navigating whatever a Wisconsin winter decides to throw at us.
